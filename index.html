The Craft & The Meaning

The braid that says
I got you.

Kemosabe — from the Ojibwe language — means "trusted friend." It is one of the oldest expressions of loyalty between people. This keychain carries that meaning forward.

Ancient hands wove braids like this to bind armor, mark devotion, and signal an unbreakable bond. 12 strands. 3 colors. Woven by Kat, by hand, one at a time. No machine. No shortcut.

Today you carry it on your keys — the thing that opens every door in your life. Every time you reach for them, you feel it. You are not alone. Your person has the match.
